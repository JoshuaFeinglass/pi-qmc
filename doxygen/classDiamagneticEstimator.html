Detailed Description

Testing estimator for diamagnetic sus.

See Pollock and Runge, "Study of diamagnetic response," J Chem Phys Vol 96, pp. ????? (1992). Eq. (7) (above equation) & Eq. 26 (which includes time-step error)

Bug:
Needs to treat particles correct at periodic boundaries.
