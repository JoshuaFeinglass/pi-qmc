Detailed Description

Select a trial move for beads by free particle sampling.

Todo:
May want to make this a subclass of Action, with a flag for whether to return probability or allow to cancel for efficiency.
Bug:

Needs to calculate the transiton probability correctly when periodic images are significant. Currently the routine neglects this.

Hard-coded for Si/Ge electron and hole.
