Detailed Description

VInd estimator for an inhomogeneous system.

We find it simplest to apply the estimator between beads, in the middle of the free particle propagator, , over the imaginary-time interval :

Even with interactions, we use the same free-particle estimator, as this estimator satisfies the continuity equation and has the correct coarse-grained response.
