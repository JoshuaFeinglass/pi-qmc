Detailed Description

Virial energy estimator.

We're going to try to subtract the following quantity,

to try to reduce the varience of the energy.

See Ceperley's review article (http://link.aps.org/abstract/RMP/v67/p279) or Herman, Bruskin, and Berne (J. Chem. Phys. 79, 5150-5155, 1983).
