Detailed Description

Class for calculating the primitive action for image charges around a dielectric slab.

The image charge potential is described in M. Kumagai, and T. Takagahara, "Excitonic and non-linear optical properties of dielectric quantum-well structures," PRB 40, 12359-12381, (1989).

The potential is given by Eqs. (2.8)-(2.15) of that paper.
